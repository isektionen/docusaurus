
# IT-Group 
We have weekly meetings where attendance is encouraged but completely voluntary. You choose what you want to spend your time on, for example:

Web Development: Help drive and further develop our section website, iare.se. Here you can try everything from frontend design to backend logic.

Physical Tech & IoT: We don’t just build digitally! We also enjoy tinkering with hardware and “smart home” solutions for the section. A great example is our traffic light system at Issy, which uses sensors to show whether the bathroom is occupied or available — a project that actually makes a difference in everyday life.

Programming Challenges: Test your skills with fun CTF (Capture The Flag) problems and small puzzles where we learn security and logic together.

Personal Projects: Got an idea for an app, a bot, or something else cool? Here you get the space and support to build your portfolio and polish your CV with exciting coding projects.
